Skills and story

I became interested in learning about programming a few years ago, but was always finding myself unable to make the time and commitment to learn.

It wasn't until May of 2019, that I found myself in a state of unhappiness while working at a retail job as an assistant manager. I wasn't happy, I was unfulfilled, and I found myself hating the fact that I was working a job I had no passion for.

It took a little bit of time, meditation, and a lot of soul searching to find what I was really passionate about. What was going to make me feel like what I was doing was fulfilling. Software development, creating, making something that I could say, "Yes, I built that."

And now, here I am, putting myself out there with things that I've learned, and created.
